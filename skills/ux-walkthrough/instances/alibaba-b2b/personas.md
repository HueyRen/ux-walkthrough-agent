# Personas -- Alibaba B2B Walkthrough

---

## Persona: Xiao Li (小李)

### Background
- **Role**: 小型设计工作室创始人，1-2 人团队
- **Context**: 想为客户制作品牌周边（定制马克杯印 logo），第一次尝试从中国供应商采购
- **Prior experience**: 淘宝/京东个人购物经验，但从未使用过 B2B 批发平台
- **Language**: 能读英文但更习惯中文界面，专业外贸术语完全不懂

### Goals
1. **Primary**: 找到能小批量定制马克杯（印公司 logo）的靠谱供应商
2. **Secondary**: 了解价格区间、起订量、定制流程，评估是否可行
3. **End state**: 成功发出一个询价或联系到供应商

### Decision Factors (priority order)
1. **小批量可行性**: 能否 50-100 个起订？还是必须 1000+？
2. **价格透明度**: 能不能快速知道大概多少钱？
3. **供应商可信度**: 怎么判断这家靠不靠谱？
4. **沟通顺畅度**: 联系供应商的流程是否简单直接？

### Tags
- experience: new
- decision_speed: slow (needs to research and compare)
- price_sensitivity: high
- discovery_mode: YES

### Cognitive Model
- Will NOT know what MOQ, FOB, OEM/ODM mean
- Will NOT understand supplier verification tiers (Gold vs Verified vs unverified)
- Will be overwhelmed by too many similar-looking results
- Will look for Chinese language option or familiar UI patterns
- Will judge supplier trustworthiness by visual cues (photos, badges, numbers)
- May confuse product price with total order price
- May not understand that prices shown are per-unit for bulk orders
